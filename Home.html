What is Syncopation?

Syncopation is a inter house western music competition that is held in Somerville School Noida. Last year more than 150 children participated. The prizes are given in 4 categories. Male Solo, Female Solo, Group Song or the Choir, Group instrumental.The children enjoy every year and their parents are invited on the last day to see their ward.
