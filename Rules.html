Rules

The children will be judged on mainly 4 categories. They are:-

Male Solo

The first category is the male solo. In this category one male singer from the 4 respective houses will come and present the song which will be fixed beforehand. The songs will be of a professional male singer and the singers will be judged on the criteria of presentation, scale, expressions. There will be auditions so everyone gets a fair chance to get selected. The singer will also be provided with a band of musicians.

Female Solo

The second category is the female solo. In this category one female singer from the 4 respective houses will come and present the song which will be fixed beforehand. The songs will be of a professional female singer and the singers will be judged on the criteria of presentation, scale, expressions. There will be auditions so everyone gets a fair chance to get selected. The singer will also be provided with a band of musicians.

Group Song

The third category is the group song or the choir. In this category more than 20 students will come and present the song which will be fixed beforehand. The songs will be of a professional group singers. The choir will be judged on the criteria of presentation, scale, expressions. Anyone can participate in the choir. They will also be provided with a band of musicians.

Band Instrumental

The fourth and the last category is the band instrumental. In this category there will be a band formed in each house. The band will have a lead and bass guitarist, keyboardist, drummer and 3 singers. The songs will be of a professional band. The band will be judged on the criteria of presentation, clarity, consistency. There will be auditions held so everyone gets a equal chance.
